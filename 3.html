Assignment 3 - Creating a drop down menu

A classic and most commonly way to hide and show elements is using the 'display' property. display: none hides the element, and display: blocks shows it. We can use this to hide and show elements. Imagine a drop down which comes up when you hover over something and goes away when you leave that element.

When you open the source, note the 'mouseenter' and 'mouseout' events that are applied to the 'Show Help!' div. We have just used 'click' for now. There are lot of such events and we will learn them one by one.
